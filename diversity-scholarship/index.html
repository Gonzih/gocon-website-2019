Diversity Scholarship

We believe that diversity is an integral part of the success to the Go community and GoCon Canada. GoCon has set aside tickets for members of underrepresented groups who would like to attend the conference. Big thanks to the sponsors who made this initiative possible.

To apply, simply complete this form before April 30th at 11:59 EST.
